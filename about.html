Operating principles

How I keep the work focused and commercially useful.

The consultancy is intentionally narrow. That is the point. It keeps the work closer to diagnosis, closer to commercial decisions, and further away from generic agency overhead.

01

Diagnose before redesigning

Recommendations should come from pressure points in the journey, not from aesthetic preference or generic best practice lists.

02

Prioritise by likely upside

The right order matters. Teams waste time when everything is presented as equally urgent or equally valuable.

03

Stay close to the decision-maker

The people commissioning the work should hear the reasoning directly, not through a diluted handoff chain.
