Samsung

Helping product comparison journeys convert with more confidence.

Ecommerce Decision friction

Commercial challenge

Strong interest and product traffic, but weaker momentum through evaluation and into checkout.

What diagnosis surfaced

  • Comparison flow was doing too little to support confident decisions
  • Trust and reassurance arrived too late in the journey
  • Mobile evaluation felt more effortful than it should have

What changed

  • Sharper product-to-product decision support
  • Stronger reassurance near commitment points
  • Cleaner progression from evaluation into purchase
Outcome shape Clearer evaluation and stronger purchase confidence.

Approved uplift, revenue impact, or testimonial can slot here without rebuilding the page structure.

P&O

Reducing hesitation through a more confident booking journey.

Travel Booking flow

Commercial challenge

Demand existed, but too much uncertainty built up before travellers reached final commitment.

What diagnosis surfaced

  • Step-to-step progression lacked confidence-building cues
  • Messaging did not consistently ease decision anxiety
  • Important actions competed with secondary information

What changed

  • Better expectation setting through the booking path
  • Stronger reassurance and journey framing
  • Clearer CTA hierarchy where decisions mattered most
Outcome shape Less booking anxiety and a smoother route to commitment.

This slot can hold the approved booking uplift, revenue delta, or a short stakeholder quote.

Disney

Aligning campaign landing intent with the action the page actually needed.

Entertainment Landing pages

Commercial challenge

High-intent campaign audiences were arriving with momentum that the landing experience did not fully carry forward.

What diagnosis surfaced

  • Message mismatch between acquisition promise and page framing
  • Important next steps were not visually prioritised enough
  • Page structure did not reflect the audience's task clearly enough

What changed

  • Tighter intent alignment from ad to landing page
  • Clearer action pathways tied to campaign goals
  • Stronger framing around the key conversion behaviour
Outcome shape Better continuity between acquisition intent and page-level action.

Use the approved figure or quote here when the engagement is ready for public-facing numbers.

F1

Clarifying high-value actions across a more complex audience journey.

Media Journey hierarchy

Commercial challenge

Multiple audience intents and multiple possible actions were diluting momentum across the experience.

What diagnosis surfaced

  • Competing actions reduced clarity around the highest-value behaviour
  • Page framing did not always support the strongest next step
  • Journey logic needed clearer prioritisation by user intent

What changed

  • Sharper action hierarchy across key touchpoints
  • Clearer pathways matched to distinct audience motivations
  • Stronger visual prioritisation of commercially important actions
Outcome shape More disciplined journey logic and less competition between actions.

Approved conversion lift, engagement improvement, or stakeholder feedback can be added here later.

Publishing proof well

There is more than one credible way to show results.

  • Approved uplift figures if numbers can be shared publicly
  • Revenue or booking impact where finance sign-off exists
  • Short testimonial excerpts if client quotes are easier to approve
  • Problem-and-intervention structure when exact metrics must stay private
